Left Center Right (LCR) Game Rules

- LCR is a multiplayer dice game for three or more players.
- The goal is to win all of the chips.

The Dice:
- Each turn involves rolling three dice.
- Each die has the letters L, C, and R, and dots on the remaining three sides.
- 'L' means passing a chip to the left.
- 'R' means passing a chip to the right.
- 'C' means placing a chip in the center pot, out of play.
- Dots are neutral and require no action.

The Chips:
- Each player starts with three chips.
- Players roll as many dice as they have chips (up to three).
- Players without chips are still in the game but do not roll.

Winning the Game:
- The last player with chips wins the game.
Enjoy!
